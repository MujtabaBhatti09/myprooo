About The Organization

Our History

Shine-On-Trust is newely launched E-Donation website Established in 2023. It is the first in our country where Donors can Donate their charity in E form. By sitting at home. Just they will have to register their selves as user and will move to their particular profile DashBoard. Where they can donate the charity in every category like eg: Food ,Clothes ,Grocery etc. And they will donate that particular donation then our rider will reach their location and will collect the donation and will be provided to Urban areas like flood victims and other homeless peoples.

Our Strategy and Mission is to keep our Organization as Non-Profit. Just to provide all donations to the needy peoples. And there are countless Charity Foundations, where some are profitable, and some are Non-Profitable. But our organization is completely online donation system, and Non-Profitable.
